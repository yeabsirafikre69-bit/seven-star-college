Admission Requirements

Join our vibrant community at Seven Star College. Here is what you need to start your journey with us.

1. Academic Qualifications: Students must have completed their 12th-grade education and meet the minimum entrance score requirement set by the Ministry of Education. We ensure our academic standards are aligned with national requirements to guarantee the quality of your certification.

2. Required Documents: Applicants are required to submit original and copies of their high school transcript, leaving certificate, and a valid identification card. Providing complete and authentic documentation is a critical step to ensure a smooth processing of your admission file.

3. Application Process: You can apply online through our official portal or visit our campus admissions office to complete the registration form in person. Our dedicated support team is available during working hours to assist you with every step of your application.

4. Entrance Assessment: Depending on the specific program you choose, applicants may need to sit for a brief entrance assessment to evaluate their readiness and academic standing. This helps us ensure that you are placed in the program best suited for your skills and career aspirations.

5. Registration Fee & Timeline: All applicants must settle the non-refundable registration fee within the announced application window to secure their spot. We encourage early application, as program capacities are limited and slots are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.
