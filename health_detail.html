Health Science Department

At Seven Star College, our Health Science department is dedicated to providing top-tier professional training. We combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical skills to ensure our graduates are ready for the healthcare industry.

Our Programs:

  • Nursing: Comprehensive care and patient management.
  • Pharmacy: Medication preparation and pharmaceutical sciences.
  • Radiology Technology: Diagnostic imaging and patient assessment.
  • Medical Laboratory Technology: Diagnostic testing and laboratory procedures.
  • Midwifery: Maternal health and newborn care.

Why Choose Us?

  • State-of-the-art laboratory facilities.
  • Experienced instructors with clinical backgrounds.
  • Strong partnerships with local hospitals for internships.
  • High employment success rate for our graduates.
